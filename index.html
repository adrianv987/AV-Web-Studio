How It Works

Simple Process, Great Results

Working with me is straightforward. Here's what to expect from first conversation to launch day.

01

Discovery

We talk through your business, your goals, who your customers are, and what your site needs to achieve. No forms — just a real conversation.

02

Design

I create a visual direction that fits your brand. You see how it'll look before a single line of code is written, and we refine until it feels right.

03

Build

The site is hand-coded — clean, fast, and built for real-world use. Every detail is considered, from loading speed to button placement.

04

Refine

You review the live site and give feedback. We make adjustments until everything is exactly right — no pressure, no rush.

05

Launch

Your site goes live. I handle the technical side — domain setup, hosting, and making sure everything is smooth from day one.
